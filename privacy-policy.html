At GagRecipe, we're committed to protecting your privacy while you enjoy our fun cooking content. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our website, you may provide us with:

  • Email addresses when subscribing to our newsletter
  • Comments and feedback on recipes
  • Poll responses and survey answers
  • Contact form submissions
  • User-generated content like recipe photos or reviews

Information Automatically Collected

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address and general location
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring websites
  • Device information (mobile, desktop, tablet)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Website Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site and improve user experience
  • Content Personalization: To show you relevant recipes and cooking content
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and send newsletters (with your consent)
  • Community Features: To enable commenting, voting, and community interactions
  • Security: To protect against spam, fraud, and other malicious activities
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Analytics and Tracking

We use the following analytics services to understand website usage:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics may use cookies to collect information such as:

  • How users navigate our website
  • Which pages are most popular
  • User demographics and interests
  • Traffic sources and referral websites

You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Plausible Analytics

We also use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics service that:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Provides aggregated website statistics only
  • Complies with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: To measure website performance and user behavior
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings and preferences
  • Social Media Cookies: For embedded content from TikTok and other platforms

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with several third-party services:

TikTok Embedded Videos

We embed TikTok videos on our website. When you view these videos, TikTok may collect information according to their privacy policy.

Social Media Integration

Our website may include social media buttons and widgets that allow you to share content. These services may track your interactions according to their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: Analytics services, hosting providers, and other technical service providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular backups and data protection procedures

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing
